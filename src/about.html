About

Thank you for using Quick Chords! I am a guitarist who always wanted to have a faster way to create slide shows and song sheets with lyrics to lead music. I also found myself reformating tabs to make them easier to read.

How it works

The "Lyric Slideshow" and "Lyric Handout" buttons remove the chords from your input and identify where the chorus/verse/etc splits are based on the section headers. Lyrics are then arranged on slides and handouts, respectively.

The "Musician Chords" button reformats your input into two columns on horizontal sheets, ideal for viewing chords both digitally and in print.

Problems?

If you are having trouble, your issue most likely has to do with your inputs. Make sure your inputs follow this format:
[verse]
G C D
line 1 of the verse
G C D
line 2 of the verse
[chorus]
G Em C
line 1 of the chorus
G Em C
line 2 of the chorus

The reformatting is done based on brackets [] and newlines
